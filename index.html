Little Enchantress

Evil monsters attacked the country of a little enchantress! Can you help her to save it?

MANUAL

When you start the game you must enter your name. You will then be asked to choose a spell.

After this the task will appear, if you do it right you hit the monster, otherwise you miss the turn.

Then monster hits you. The move goes to you.

If the monster is killed, you can go to the next level, where health will increase slightly.
